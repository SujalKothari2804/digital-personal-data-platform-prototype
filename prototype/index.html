Your rights under DPDP Act 2023

• §11 — Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (72-hour delivery).

• §12 — Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data (30-day review).

• §13 — Erasure: Request deletion of your account and personal data.

• §6 — Consent withdrawal: Withdraw any optional consent at any time via Privacy Centre → Manage Consent. Withdrawal does not affect lawfulness of prior processing.

• Grievance: Email privacy@slicebank.in. Response within 90 days per DPDP Rules.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Breach notification is provided to CERT-In and RBI within required timelines, and to the Data Protection Board per DPDP Act requirements.

Children's data

slice does not knowingly collect data from users under 18. Per DPDP Act 2023, verifiable parental consent is required for all minors. If you believe a minor's data has been collected without consent, contact privacy@slicebank.in immediately.
